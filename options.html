Search City (optional)
  • If you will be searching in multiple cities, leave this blank.
  • Walk Score's engine deals very well with incomplete addresses and neighborhood names, often guessing correctly. However, sometimes a search string is truly ambiguous, like 'Beacon Hill', for example, which could be in Boston or Seattle. Entering a search city will help in these situations. This extension will search for each word entered here and append it to the end of the search if it does not exist already in the selected text. For this reason, an unqualified city name will usually work best (so enter Melbourne rather than Melbourne, Victoria, Australia).
  • For multiple-word tokens, use quotes. (So enter "New York" rather than New York, and Sydney "New South Wales" rather than Sydney New South Wales)
